About Me

I started my career as a Environmental Scientist but began transitioning to web development while working as a QA Analyst in San Francisco. I was really excited to see features that I had tested make it into production code.

I can't wait to collaborate with other developers to build cool and exciting software that:

  2. Helps people plan better
  3. Helps people track progress

When I am not coding I love to play basketball, bike and write scientific articles on climate change and sustainability.
