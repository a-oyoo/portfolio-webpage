About Me

Becoming a software developer has always been my dream. However with a PhD in Environmental Sciences, I began my career modeling impacts of climate change adaptation and mitigation on the environment.

Whereas this was also an extremely fulfilling career, I renewed my interest in Software Engineering while working as a QA Analyst in San Francisco. I was really excited to see features that I had tested make it into production code. I am really glad to have finally made this step towards becoming a software developer.

I am learning to code in Javascript to develop websites and in Python and SQL to process and analyse datasets from relational databases.

I can't wait to start building cool features with other programmers.

When I am not coding I love to play basketball, bike, ride a motorcycle and write scientific articles on climate change and sustainability.
