About Me

Becoming a software developer has always been my dream. However with a PhD in Environmental Sciences, I began my career modeling impacts of climate change adaptation on mitigation on the enviromnent. Whereas this was also extremely fulfilling I developed a renewed interest in Software Engineering while working as a QA Analyst in San Francisco. I was really excited to see features that I had tested make it into production code. I am really glad to have finally made the steps towards becoming a software developer.

I code in Python and SQL to process and analyse datasets from relational databases and JavaScript for web development.

I studied in Europe but finished my post-graduate studies at UC Berkeley.

When I am not coding I love to play basketball, bike, ride a motorcycle and write scientific articles on climate change and sustainability.
